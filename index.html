WHO ARE WE?

AS SALAMU ALIKUM WA RAHMATULAHI WA BARKATUH.

All praise is due to Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Compassionate. May the blessings and peace of Allah be upon our beloved Prophet Muhammad, his family, his companions, and those who follow in his guidance.

Welcome to our website! We are a community of students of knowledge who started on social media, particularly through our Islamic WhatsApp group (join our WhatsApp group). Our aim is to create a platform where people can learn about Islam from authentic scholars' videos and reliable sources, ensuring that the teachings of our faith, upon the understanding of Salaf, are accurately conveyed and easily accessible.

We recognize that the internet can be a vast and sometimes confusing place, with many differing opinions and interpretations. It is our goal to provide a clear and trustworthy resource for those seeking to deepen their understanding of Islam. Our community values the knowledge and guidance of established salafi scholars and strives to share that knowledge in a way that is accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or level of expertise.

This website is our humble effort to bridge the gap between scholars and laymen, making it easier for anyone to learn about Islam in a safe and reliable environment. You will find articles, videos, and other resources that focus on the core teachings of Islam, as well as practical advice on how to implement those teachings in your daily life.

We believe that by connecting people with authentic Islamic knowledge, we can help them grow in their faith upon the understanding of Salaf, strengthen their relationship with Allah, and contribute positively to their communities. We are excited to have you here and look forward to sharing this journey of learning and growth with you.
